Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the Site to function properly (e.g., to remember your cookie preferences).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the Site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense and other ad networks to serve personalized ads based on your interests.
  • Functionality cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 First-Party Cookies

These are set by aivstool.com directly. They include essential cookies needed for site functionality.

3.2 Third-Party Cookies

These are set by domains other than aivstool.com, such as:

  • Google (AdSense): For serving ads and measuring ad performance. Google's cookie policy.
  • Google Analytics: For understanding Site traffic. Google's privacy policy.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse all cookies or to refuse third-party cookies. The methods for doing so vary from browser to browser, and from version to version. You can obtain up-to-date information about blocking and deleting cookies via these links:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

Opt-out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (US)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Google Ad Settings

5. Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Site from the EU/EEA or UK, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences. We will only set non-essential cookies if you provide consent.

Note: The cookie consent banner will be implemented when we add a consent management platform (CMP) such as Cookiebot or Complianz.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
